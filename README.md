# Human Companions – Revived

A **NeoForge 1.21.1** revival of [Human Companions](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/human-companions) by justinwon777. This version is maintained by **ShadowedBlue** and targets NeoForge 1.21.1 only. The revival was developed with the help of AI.

Original mod: [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/human-companions) · Licensed under [GPL v3](LICENSE).

---

## About

This mod adds human companions that can help you on your journey. Find them in the wild by their house, tame them with food, and they’ll fight for you and carry items.

## Features

- **Companion types:** Knight, Archer, Axeguard, and Arbalist (crossbow)
- Find them in the wild by their house
- Give them food to have them join you
- They fight for you and can carry items (avoid storing valuables—they can die)
- **Modes:** Follow, patrol an area, or guard

## New in this revival

- **Companion Bed:** Bind a companion to a bed for respawn after a minecraft day, or use a golden apple to respawn immediately, and recall them to the bed with a stick.
- **Companion Recall:** Craftable item (stick + diamond) to bind (Shift + Right-click) and recall a companion to you
- **Structure biome overrides:** Config to add modded biomes (e.g. Biomes O’ Plenty) so companion houses can spawn there

## Quick Guide

Companions can have different looks and live in a variety of houses across biomes.

- **Right-click** to see what food they want. Give them that food to tame them.
- **Right-click** once tamed to open their inventory.
- They have names; you can rename them with name tags.
- **Shift + right-click** to toggle “sit” (stay in place).
- Give them armor and weapons; they equip the best in their inventory. They only use the weapon type they spawn with. Archers and arbalists don’t need arrows.
- They follow like wolves and attack mobs that hurt you or that you attack (except creepers; they warn and flee from creepers).
- Put food in their inventory and they’ll eat to heal.
- Toggle **Hunt** and **Alert** in their inventory to control behavior.
- Toggle **Stationery** to keep archers/arbalists standing while shooting.
- Use `/locate structure humancompanions:companion_house` to find a companion house.

## Known Issues

**Note:** This revival has not had extensive testing; there may be bugs not listed here. Please report issues on the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/shadowedblue/HumanCompanions-Revived).

- When a companion dies, items may sometimes duplicate or not drop correctly.
- Archers may occasionally shoot at nothing after killing a mob; use the clear target button.
- Spawn eggs can rarely spawn a baby that can’t act and may despawn on reload.
- Knights can sometimes hesitate while attacking.
